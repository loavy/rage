SUNNY ROOM GAMES / PRIVATE NOTES

the homepage is the mask.
the games are memories with score counters removed.
the mascot is not evil. it is tired from smiling.
the room appears when language fails.

do not explain the whole thing to the player.
let them notice the wrongness first.

if they find enough fragments, let the boy speak plainly for a moment.
not perfectly.
just enough to prove he is still there.
